Write for World Jungle Tales

Share Your Voice. Inspire Conservation.

Why Contribute?

At World Jungle Tales, we believe stories have the power to protect the wild. We're inviting writers, conservationists, educators, and nature lovers to help us inspire change — one tale at a time. Whether it's a jungle myth, a conservation essay, or a personal story, your voice matters.

What We’re Looking For

  • 🌱 First-person conservation experiences
  • 🐾 Jungle folklore and cultural storytelling
  • 📸 Wildlife photo essays
  • 🌍 Indigenous knowledge and oral traditions
  • 📚 Educational content (e.g. for children, schools)
  • ✍️ Fiction or creative nonfiction with a nature focus

Submission Guidelines

  • Word count: 800–2,000 words
  • Tone: Engaging, respectful, educational
  • Originality: Content must be original and unpublished
  • Images: Optional, must be royalty-free or your own
  • Language: English (for now)

What Contributors Get

  • 📣 Author bio + link to your website or organization
  • 🌍 Exposure to a global, conservation-minded audience
  • 🦉 Feature in our newsletter & social platforms
  • 🏆 Chance to be spotlighted as “Story of the Month”

Editorial & Publishing Policy

We may lightly edit submissions for clarity, grammar, or house style. By submitting, you confirm the work is original and you grant World Jungle Tales permission to publish and promote your article. You retain full ownership of your content.

How to Submit

Please send your article (as a Word document, Google Doc link, or plain text), along with:

  • Your full name
  • A short author bio (2–3 lines)
  • Any images you'd like to include (attach separately)
  • Links to your website or social profiles (optional)

✉️ Send your submission to: editor@worldjungletales.com

We review all submissions within 5–10 business days. If selected, we’ll get in touch with publication details. Thank you for your interest in sharing your voice with World Jungle Tales!
